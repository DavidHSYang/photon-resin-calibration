# ANYCUBIC RESIN EXPOSURE FINDER by X3msnake

---------------------------------------------

### PREPARATION ###

Download and unzip your folder
Copy resin-xp-finder folder to your machine USB


### SETUP ###

- Make sure your machine is levelled before running the test
- Insert and fix your vat and build platform
- Load your Vat with with your test resin


### ENABLE MACHINE TEST MODE ###

- Run test-mode.gcode


### EXPOSE YOUR TEST RESIN ###

- Run resin-test-005.100.2.20.photon


### CLEAN AND CURE FILM ###

- Remove your Build Plate
- Rinse with your resin cleaning Sovent (Usually IPA)
- Cleanup your plate
- Cure your test Film still in plate
- After curing you can gently peel off your film from the plate


### MAKING SENSE OF THE RESULTS ###

- To know what exposure time each column represents, just multiply the column number by 2
- Resin minimum exposure: Look for the first column were the cured graphics begin to show 
- Resin sweet spot: find the first column where you can see most of the details the one just before the negative spaces start to disapear
- Resin overexposure: The negative space starts to disapear
- Resin underexposure: The small lines and details are missing 


### RETURN YOUR MACHINE TO PRINT MODE ###

- Run print-mode.gcode


---------------------------------------------

That's It, dial in your new found settings on a test piece and enjoy the magic
